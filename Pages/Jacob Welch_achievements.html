My Projects

A list of some of my best projects I've made over the years.

Cybershot

Cybershot is 5-week pvp parkour first-person bow shooter. In it, the player can go for stylish outplays by flexing on their opponents with superior movement techs. This project took a lot of work learning how to network video games and how to implement fun first-person combat. By clicking the link below, you will be able to play the game!

Wicked Wizards

Wicked Wizards is currently my capstone project for my 38-person team. It is based on the prototype I worked on called Wizards of Wizardly. The game is a 4-player CO-OP Towerd Defense FPS where you play as evil wizards against an onslaught of cute fairytale creatures. It is a bloody, gory mess that you won't want to miss!

Constellation Exploration

Constellation Exploration is a VR Constellation learning experience. It was developed by me and 9 other developers over the course of a semester for our schools spring expo. The biggest selling point for this project was our accuracte sky map that had all of the stars place relative to how they would actually look in the night sky.

Zero Gravity

Zero Gravity is a 5-week prototype I worked on with 5 other developers. It features a flying third-person shooter in space. The world is procedurally generated and the player has a set of three abilities they can utilize. There isn't much gameplay content as it was a mechanical prototype.
